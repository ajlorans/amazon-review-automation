Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 19, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Amazon Review Video Automation application ("the Application") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

The Application operates locally on your device and collects minimal information:

  • API Credentials: Your platform API keys and access tokens are stored locally in configuration files on your device
  • Video Files: Video files you process are stored locally in the Application's storage folders
  • Configuration Data: Application settings and preferences stored in your local .env file

3. How We Use Your Information

All information collected by the Application is used solely for:

  • Processing and formatting your video content
  • Authenticating with social media platform APIs
  • Uploading content to your social media accounts as drafts

We do not transmit, share, or sell any of your information to third parties.

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device:

  • API credentials: storage/credentials/ and storage/tokens/
  • Video files: storage/inputs/, storage/outputs/, and storage/archive/
  • Configuration: .env file in the application root

No data is stored on remote servers or transmitted to external services except when uploading to the social media platforms you authorize.

5. Third-Party Services

The Application integrates with the following third-party services:

  • YouTube API: For uploading videos to YouTube. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • Instagram Graph API: For uploading videos to Instagram. Subject to Meta's Privacy Policy
  • TikTok Content Publishing API: For uploading videos to TikTok. Subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy

When you use these integrations, your content and API credentials are transmitted directly to these platforms according to their respective privacy policies.

6. Data Security

We implement the following security measures:

  • All sensitive files (credentials, tokens) are excluded from version control via .gitignore
  • API credentials are stored in local files that should not be shared
  • No network transmission of credentials except for API authentication

You are responsible for:

  • Keeping your .env file and credential files secure
  • Not committing sensitive files to public repositories
  • Using strong, unique API credentials

7. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:

  • You can delete any stored data at any time by deleting the relevant files
  • You can revoke API access by removing tokens or revoking permissions in platform settings
  • You can uninstall the Application, which removes all local data (except files you've explicitly saved elsewhere)

8. Children's Privacy

The Application is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when changes were made.

10. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the application developer through the project repository.
